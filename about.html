About OpenPage

Our Mission

OpenPage is a free digital library with a simple mission: make the world's literature accessible to everyone, no matter their device, connection speed, or budget.

Every book is available to read online instantly — no account needed, no paywall, no tracking, no friction. Just you and the words.

Where the Books Come From

All books on OpenPage are sourced from Project Gutenberg — a volunteer effort to digitize and archive cultural works that are in the public domain. We're grateful for their incredible catalog of over 70,000 free ebooks.

We also plan to integrate other open-licensed collections in the future.

Design Principles

  • Performance first. Our site loads fast even on 3G connections in developing regions. Every kilobyte counts.
  • Mobile-first responsive. Designed to work beautifully on a $30 Android phone just as well as a desktop monitor.
  • Accessible for all. Semantic HTML, ARIA labels, keyboard navigation, and screen-reader-friendly content.
  • No JavaScript required to read. Book content is always available as plain text. JS enhances but never gates the experience.
  • Privacy-respecting. No cookies, no tracking, no accounts. We don't need to know who you are.

Privacy

OpenPage does not use cookies, collect personal data, or require accounts. We serve books directly from Project Gutenberg's CDN and minimize external dependencies. Future features like donations may use third-party services (Ko-fi, Buy Me a Coffee) that have their own privacy policies.

Support OpenPage

If you find value in OpenPage, consider supporting us:

  • Share OpenPage with a friend or student who loves reading.
  • Donate — contributions help us cover hosting and development costs. Support on Ko-fi
  • Contribute — OpenPage is open-source. Help us improve the code, fix bugs, or add features.
